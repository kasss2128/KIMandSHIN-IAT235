Lampe de Bureau Table Lamp

The Jean Prouvé Lampe de Bureau Table Lamp is a classic example of modernist industrial design. Its design is simple, functional, and elegant, reflecting Prouvé's background in metalworking and engineering.

The lamp's powder-coated folded sheet steel and aluminum construction gives it a lightweight and industrial feel, while the curved steel tube arm and Bakelite knob provide a distinctive touch.

The lamp is designed to be adjustable, allowing the user to direct the light where it is needed. The lamp's shade is also adjustable, with a small knob on the side of the shade that allows it to pivot up and down. This versatility makes the lamp ideal for use as a reading light, task light, or general illumination in a variety of settings.

The Jean Prouvé Lampe de Bureau Table Lamp is considered a classic of modernist design and is still produced today by the Swiss lighting company Vitra, reflecting the enduring appeal of Prouvé's functional and timeless designs.
